About Project

This project uses Machine Learning to recommend the most suitable crop based on soil nutrients and environmental conditions such as Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium, Temperature, Humidity, pH and Rainfall. It helps farmers make better agricultural decisions and improve crop productivity.
